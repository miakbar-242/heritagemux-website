What is Google Classroom?

A free, safe platform connecting your child with their teacher — lessons, assignments and feedback, all from your phone.

Watch Lessons Anytime

Teachers record short video lessons your child can watch at home, at whatever time suits your family. Videos stay available to rewatch.

Receive Assignments

Work and learning materials are posted in the app. You get an automatic notification the moment something new is available.

Submit Completed Work

Photograph your child's completed work and upload it in seconds. No printing or scanning needed — just your phone camera.

Receive Teacher Feedback

The teacher reviews the submitted work, leaves comments or marks, and sends it back — so you always know how your child is doing.

How a Lesson Works

The same simple process applies to every class — from the moment the teacher records a lesson to the moment feedback reaches you.

1
All Classes

Teacher records and posts the lesson

Your child's teacher records a short video lesson — 5 to 15 minutes — and posts it in Google Classroom with the day's task or worksheet. The video stays available to rewatch at any time.

2
All Classes

You receive a notification

The moment a new lesson is posted, you will receive an email notification. Open Google Classroom to find the video and the day's instructions.

3
Montessori Parents — Mont 1, 2 & 3

Watch together and do the activity with your child

Sit with your child, watch the lesson video together, and help them complete the hands-on activity — tracing, sorting, counting, colouring, or whatever the teacher has set. When done, take a clear photo of the completed work. Your involvement at this age makes all the difference.

3
Primary Parents — Class 1 to 5

Your child watches the lesson and completes the work

Your child watches the lesson and then completes the assigned work in their notebook, textbook, or on a worksheet. When finished, take a clear photo of the completed pages.

4
All Classes

Submit the work through Google Classroom

Open the assignment in the app, tap Add Attachment, upload the photo, then tap Turn In. Done — the teacher is notified straight away.

5
All Classes

Teacher reviews and sends feedback

Your child's teacher reviews the submitted work, adds an encouraging comment or grade, and clicks Return. You get a notification — open the assignment to read the feedback and celebrate your child's effort! 🌟
